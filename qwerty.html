How It Works

Not your keys, not your crypto... right? We take that concept to the next level.

This offline-only (browser-based) tool uses algorithms that produce a highly randomized pair of alphanumeric characters. Each pair represents 1 character of your private key. It doesn't double your security, it multiplies it by 3.101×10^97. It makes your encoded keys unreadable and exponentially increases your backup security. Then we add 256-bit AES encryption on top.

Only by re-combining the keymap and encoded keys can you reveal your full private keys. It makes your key storage highly resistant to theft, and easy to recover in the event of a disaster.
